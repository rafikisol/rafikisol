### Rafikisol ###
## What is Rafikisol?##
Rafikisol (solemn friend) is meant to combine Google Earth Engine (GEE), rgee, tidymodels and more into models to use
for everyone who might need them. We are open to new ideas and constructive critisim. Our own ideas are free to do with what you may.
However, we ask you please cite the original paper/idea when needed. We believe that open source information is not a luxury, its info, 
therefore if the code does not provide enough information or something is wrong please contact us. Lets improve, not waste the knowledge we know.
