# Rafikisol
## What is Rafikisol?
Rafikisol (solum friend) is meant to combine Google Earth Engine (GEE), rgee, tidymodels and more into models to use
for everyone who might need them. We take original and new ideas for mapping environmental questions and we are open to new ideas and constructive critisim. Our own ideas are free to do with what you may,
however, we ask you please cite the original paper/idea when needed. Since packages have usually a quick and direct description (as do ours) we encourge people to ask why/how, what purpose it has or whatever is on ones mind. We encourage feedback and if someone wants to collaborate, reach out and we will make something meaningful.
